## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the sales performance of **BrightMart Retail Group** using Excel, SQL Server, and Power BI.

The objective is to investigate why the company's profitability is declining despite maintaining strong sales performance and to provide data-driven recommendations for management.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The management team observed that:

- Sales remain relatively stable.
- Profit growth is slower than revenue growth.
- Performance varies significantly across branches and sellers.
- Some customers have become inactive.

The company requested a complete business analysis to identify the root causes and support strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall company performance.
- Evaluate branch performance.
- Evaluate seller performance.
- Analyze product and category profitability.
- Identify high-revenue but low-profit products.
- Identify inactive customers.
- Build an Executive Dashboard.
- Provide business insights and recommendations.

## 💡 Key Insights

- Overall company performance was evaluated over time.
- Branch performance varies significantly.
- Some products generate high revenue but low profit margins.
- Product categories contribute differently to profitability.
- Several customers have not purchased for more than 90 days.
- Seller performance differs across branches.

---

## 📋 Business Recommendations

- Review pricing strategy for low-margin products.
- Reduce product costs where possible.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-margin products.
- Launch campaigns to reactivate inactive customers.
- Monitor underperforming branches and sellers.
- Continue tracking profitability using the Power BI dashboard.
